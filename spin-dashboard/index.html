Spin Dashboard

Driving data-informed action for a micromobility company

Duration

Oct - Nov 2021 (4 wks)

Client

Spin (sponsored by Carnegie Mellon University)

Team

3 designers — Heying Wang, Sree Mahit Munakala, Sally Chen

Skills

Interaction Design, Data-Driven Displays, Problem Definition, Current & Future State Modeling, Personas, Animations, Pitch Presentation

Overview

Spin is a micromobility company that is reimagining the way people and cities flow. With over 70 partnerships around the world, the Spin app provides communities with access to rentable electric scooters.

On this project, I designed an interactive dashboard of Spin scooter data that could be plausibly used by both Spin gig workers and data analysts—two groups with potentially divergent user goals. I worked with my team of interaction designers to identify a hero display and the accompanying UI widgets that would serve these two user groups' shared need for efficiency.

I was responsible for a major portion of the information architecture, visual consistency, and animations that you see in the final Figma prototype below. Click here to view a comprehensive video demo of the prototype by yours truly (sorry in advance for the goofy intro).
