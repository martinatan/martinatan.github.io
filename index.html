--- layout: default title: Martina Tan, artist & designer ---

I’m Martina Tan, a designer and artist based in Boston, Massachusetts.

I'm from Mount Laurel, New Jersey, but I currently split my time between Tufts and the School of the Museum of Fine Arts as a fifth-year Combined Degree student. In 2021, I’ll be graduating with two degrees in Cognitive and Brain Science and Visual Art, plus a Computer Science minor.

Design and art have been my most persistent passions since elementary school, when I would jump at any chance to meticulously sketch and hand-write all of my project posters. I still love all manners of creating by hand, but I've also expanded my toolbox into the digital realms of programming and prototyping. I hope to pursue a career in Human-Computer Interaction or UX Design while maintaining a steady art practice.

I find joy in collaborating with people of diverse backgrounds throughout my design process, with the goal of achieving solutions that are audience-conscious and accessible.
