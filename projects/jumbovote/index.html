JumboVote

I designed a logo featuring our school mascot for a nonpartisan organization that promotes voter participation by making voting resources accessible from campus.

Final logo design (Click for full view)

  • time range: July 2018
  • client: Tisch College of Civic Life, JumboVote initiative
  • tools: Sketch, FireAlpaca

This was the winning entry in a logo design competition held by JumboVote, with a small cash prize. The prompt required that the logo involve Jumbo (the Tufts mascot), the text "JumboVote", and a circular design. I appreciated the specificity because it allowed me to quickly send in a rough sketch for feedback and spend more time polishing it.

I most enjoyed designing Jumbo here, since he can range from cute to fearsome depending on context. My sketch defaulted to a rounded and cutesy elephant, but I changed it to a more naturalistic profile while maintaining a simple "lineless" look, which I feel better parallels the JumboVote mission and image.
