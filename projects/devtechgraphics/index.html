DevTech Promotional Flyers

As a summer intern at the DevTech Research Group, I revamped informational flyers and postcards that promote the lab's work and programs in early childhood technology.

Postcard for the ECT Certificate Program (Click for full view)

  • time range: July 2019
  • client: DevTech Research Group
  • tools: Sketch, Adobe Illustrator

This was an exercise in re-designing a set of flyers and postcards that had been created in Microsoft Office. Not only did it involve formatting the text based on how the information should be arranged logically and hierarchically, but it also involved some re-synthesizing of the information so that the most pertinent details could fit into a certain amount of space on the page.
