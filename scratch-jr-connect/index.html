Challenge

How might we allow educators to access DevTech-approved teaching materials related to ScratchJr?

This project was inactive at DevTech for some years before I took it on as an independent study in order to apply UX design skills that I had primarily learned outside of classes.

My goal was to create a functional website that allows users to submit resources pertaining to ScratchJr, which is a tablet-based application for children ages 4-7 years old to learn to program while exercising their creativity and making engaging animations. The website needed to be scalable to serve possibly millions of existing educators who teach using ScratchJr in their classrooms or at home.
